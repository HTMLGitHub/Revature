Homemade Hemp Milk

Ingredientes

  • 1/4 cup Raw Unshelled Hemp Seeds
  • 4 cups Water
  • 1/4 tsp of sea salt
  • 1-2 maple syrup

Directions

  1. Add all ingredientes to a high speed blender
  2. Blend on high for about 1-2 minutes
  3. Store in fridge for up to 5 days
    If you let it sit for more than 5 days, it will go bad
